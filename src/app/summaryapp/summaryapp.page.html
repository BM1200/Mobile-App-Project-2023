Project Summary Project Summary

For this project I studied four of the most popular phone distributors of the past decade. There are 4 pages for each phone company that gives a brief summary of their history and impact on our culture.

There are four major features of this app, one is a GPS that displays your current location, one saves your favourite phone company from a radio button, one is an API that lists names and posters of movies and finally the fourth feature gets you current cinema times for Salthill Omniplex
