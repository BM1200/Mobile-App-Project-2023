Blackberry (Brief Overview)

The BlackBerry was a line of smartphones developed by Canadian company, Research In Motion (RIM), which gained popularity in the early 2000s due to their advanced security and messaging features. However, as the smartphone market evolved, the company struggled to keep up and eventually stopped producing phones in 2016, pivoting to software and services.
