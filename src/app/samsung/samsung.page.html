Samsung (Brief Overview)

Samsung is a South Korean electronics company that has been producing smartphones since 2009. Their Galaxy series of phones have become a popular alternative to Apple's iPhone, with features such as large screens, high-quality cameras, and customizable interfaces. Samsung phones run on the Android operating system and are known for their advanced technology and innovative design.
