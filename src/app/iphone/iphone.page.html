iPhone (Brief Overview)

The iPhone is a smartphone developed by Apple Inc. and first released in 2007. It has since become one of the most popular and iconic smartphones in the world, known for its sleek design and user-friendly interface. The iPhone has undergone numerous updates and revisions over the years, with new models released annually.
